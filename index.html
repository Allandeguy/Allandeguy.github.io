Batman

Basic Info

Batman is a fictional superhero created by artist Bob Kane and writer Bill Finger, first appearing in Detective Comics #27 in 1939. By day, he is Bruce Wayne, a wealthy businessman and philanthropist in Gotham City; by night, he becomes Batman, a masked vigilante who fights crime using his intellect, detective skills, martial arts expertise, and a wide array of high-tech gadgets. Secret Identity

Bruce Wayne is a billionaire industrialist and philanthropist who lives in the fictional city of Gotham. Orphaned as a child after witnessing the murder of his parents, he grows up haunted by their loss and determined to fight crime. Using his wealth, intelligence, and physical training, Bruce adopts the secret identity of Batman to protect Gotham from criminals and corruption. Despite his public image as a charming and carefree playboy, Bruce is deeply driven, often struggling with the emotional weight of his dual life as both a man and a symbol of justice.
